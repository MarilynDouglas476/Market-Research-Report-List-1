<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) Communication Technology encompasses several key market types, including transmitters, receivers, modulators, demodulators, and others. Transmitters convert electrical signals into optical signals for free-space transmission. Receivers capture and convert optical signals back into electrical form. Modulators manipulate the optical signal’s properties for efficient data encoding, while demodulators extract the original data from the received signal. Other components may include optics and alignment tools, which enhance system performance and reliability in various communication applications.</p></p>
