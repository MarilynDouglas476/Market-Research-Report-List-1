<p><p>The medical micro molding market is categorized into several types based on materials used. PEEK (polyether ether ketone) is known for its high strength and biocompatibility, making it suitable for implants. Bioabsorbable materials are designed to dissolve within the body over time, ideal for temporary devices. Silicone is favored for its flexibility and durability, often used in seals and gaskets. Rubber offers excellent elasticity and is used in a variety of applications. The "Others" category includes diverse materials catering to specific medical needs.</p></p>
