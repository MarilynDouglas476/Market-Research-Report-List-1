<p><strong>The Bio-based Renewable Jet Fuel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HEFA-SPK</li><li>FT-SPK</li><li>ATJ-SPK</li><li>Co-processing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-based renewable jet fuel market comprises several types, each with unique production processes. HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is derived from various fats and oils. FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) uses gasification of biomass. ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) converts alcohols into jet fuel. Co-processing integrates bio-feedstocks into existing petroleum refineries. Other emerging methods include generated fuels from non-food biomass and waste materials, contributing to sustainable aviation fuels.</p></p>
