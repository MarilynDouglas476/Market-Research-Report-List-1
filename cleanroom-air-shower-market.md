<p><p>The Cleanroom Air Shower market primarily consists of two types based on filter options: HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters are designed to capture 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them suitable for many cleanroom applications. In contrast, ULPA filters provide a higher efficiency, trapping 99.999% of particles down to 0.1 microns, ideal for environments requiring stricter contamination control, such as pharmaceuticals and electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
