RF Filters eliminate unwanted frequencies, ensuring cleaner signals, and Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA) enhance weak signals without adding significant noise. Other devices may include components like duplexers and antenna modules, supporting overall network functionality and performance. Together, these elements are crucial for the seamless operation of 5G networks.</p></p>
