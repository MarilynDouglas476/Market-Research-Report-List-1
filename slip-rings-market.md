<p><ul><li>FSC</li><li>FSH</li><li>FSK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slip rings market is categorized into three main types: FSC (Fiber Slip Ring), FSH (Hollow Slip Ring), and FSK (Conductive Slip Ring). FSC slip rings ensure high-speed data transmission through fiber optics, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal signal loss. FSH slip rings are designed with a hollow structure, allowing for the passage of cables or fluids, suitable for space-constrained environments. FSK slip rings utilize conductive materials for efficient electrical connections, commonly used in industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
