<p><p>The Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) market is categorized into ported and non-ported types. Ported short PIVC features an additional port for facilitating multiple infusions or blood sampling without removing the catheter, improving convenience and efficiency in patient care. Non-ported short PIVC, on the other hand, lacks this accessory port, making it simpler and typically used for straightforward infusions. Both types are essential in various medical settings, each serving specific clinical needs depending on the procedure or treatment plan.</p></p>
