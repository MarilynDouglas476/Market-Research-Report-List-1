<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) resin market is primarily divided into two types: phosgene type and non-phosgene type. Phosgene type PC is produced using phosgene as a key reagent, offering excellent thermal stability and optical clarity, making it suitable for applications like automotive and electronics. In contrast, non-phosgene type PC is synthesized using alternative methods, thus avoiding the use of toxic phosgene. This type is increasingly favored for its environmental safety and is gaining traction in various industries, including packaging and consumer goods.</p></p>
