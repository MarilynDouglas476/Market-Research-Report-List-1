<p><p>The Hem Flange Adhesive & Sealing Market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component systems are pre-formulated and ready to use, providing convenience and ease in application. They typically cure when exposed to air or heat. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before use, offering superior bonding strength and durability. This type is preferred for applications needing enhanced performance and resistance under various conditions.</p></p>
