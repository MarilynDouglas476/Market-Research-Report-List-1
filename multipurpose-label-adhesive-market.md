<p><p>The multipurpose label adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives are known for their low environmental impact, fast drying time, and ease of cleaning, making them suitable for various applications, including food packaging. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives offer superior adhesion strength and resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for demanding environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including retail, logistics, and consumer goods, ensuring reliable bonding solutions.</p></p>
