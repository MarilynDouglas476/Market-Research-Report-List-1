<p><ul><li>Wet Drill Bits</li><li>Dry Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction industry core drill market is primarily divided into two types of drill bits: wet and dry. Wet drill bits utilize a coolant, typically water, to reduce heat and remove debris, making them ideal for materials like concrete and masonry. This method often results in cleaner cuts and extends the life of the drill bit. Conversely, dry drill bits operate without coolant, making them suitable for softer materials and environments where moisture is not feasible, promoting portability and convenience.</p></p>
