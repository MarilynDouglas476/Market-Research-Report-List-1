<p><p>The fire detection sensors market is categorized into wired and wireless systems. Wired sensors connect directly to a centralized alarm system, offering reliable performance and constant power supply, making them suitable for larger infrastructures. Conversely, wireless sensors provide flexibility in installation and are easier to retrofit in existing buildings, eliminating the need for extensive wiring. While wired systems are typically more robust, wireless options are increasingly popular for residential applications due to their convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
