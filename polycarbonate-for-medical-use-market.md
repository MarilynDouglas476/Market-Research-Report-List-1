<p><p>Polycarbonate for medical use is categorized into two main types: Phosgene type and Non-Phosgene type. The Phosgene type is produced using phosgene as an essential precursor, which enhances its mechanical and thermal properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, the Non-Phosgene type employs alternative synthesis methods, often resulting in a more environmentally friendly process. Both types are utilized in various medical applications, including devices, casing, and sterile packaging, due to their biocompatibility and durability.</p></p>
