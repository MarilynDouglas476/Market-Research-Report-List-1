<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The I/O relays market is primarily categorized into electromagnetic relays and solid-state relays. Electromagnetic relays use mechanical components to open or close circuits, providing reliable switching for various applications but typically have slower response times and limited lifespan. In contrast, solid-state relays utilize semiconductor technology, enabling faster switching and increased durability with no moving parts. Both types serve crucial roles in automation, control systems, and industrial applications, catering to different requirements based on speed, reliability, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
