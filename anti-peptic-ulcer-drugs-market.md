<p><p>The anti-peptic ulcer drugs market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs)** reduce stomach acid production, facilitating ulcer healing.</p><p>2. **Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB)** offer an alternative mechanism for suppressing acid secretion.</p><p>3. **Antacids** neutralize existing stomach acid, providing quick relief from symptoms.</p><p>4. **H2 Antagonists** decrease acid production by blocking histamine receptors.</p><p>5. **Ulcer Protective Drugs** form a protective barrier on the ulcer surface, promoting healing and protecting against irritants. Each type targets acid-related disorders differently.</p></p>
