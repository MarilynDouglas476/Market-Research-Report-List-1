<p><p>The Bipolar Temporary Pacing Catheters market is divided into two main types: those without balloons and those with balloons. Temporary pacing catheters without balloons are designed for straightforward cardiac pacing, providing electrical stimulation without additional hemodynamic support. In contrast, catheters with balloons offer the dual function of pacing and potentially enhancing blood flow, as the balloon can occlude blood vessels to assist in managing hemodynamic stability. Both types are critical for various clinical scenarios in cardiac care.</p></p>
