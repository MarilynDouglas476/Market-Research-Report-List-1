<p><p>Infrared diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors are categorized into two main market types: digital signal and analog signal sensors. Digital signal sensors provide discrete output, indicating whether an object is present or absent, which is ideal for applications needing simple on/off detection. On the other hand, analog signal sensors offer continuous output proportional to the distance or intensity of the reflected infrared light, allowing for more precise measurements. Both types are widely used in industrial automation and safety systems.</p></p>
