<p><p>The MEMS microphone transducer market is primarily divided into two types: analog MEMS microphones and digital MEMS microphones. Analog MEMS microphones convert sound waves into analog signals, which are then processed by external circuits. They are known for their simplicity and low power consumption. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones output signals in a digital format, integrating digital signal processing for enhanced performance and noise reduction. This differentiation caters to various applications, from consumer electronics to industrial use.</p></p>
