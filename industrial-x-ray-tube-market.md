<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary X-ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode X-ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Tube Market comprises primarily two types: Stationary X-ray Tubes and Rotating Anode X-ray Tubes. Stationary X-ray Tubes have a fixed anode, making them suitable for lower-power applications and less demanding environments. Conversely, Rotating Anode X-ray Tubes feature an anode that spins during operation, allowing for higher heat dissipation and making them ideal for high-energy applications. This differentiation influences their performance, cost, and suitability across various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and non-destructive testing.</p></p>
