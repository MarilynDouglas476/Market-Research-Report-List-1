<p><p>The modified bitumen waterproof membrane market primarily consists of two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes are known for their elastomeric properties, offering flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures, making them suitable for a variety of climates. In contrast, APP membranes provide enhanced UV resistance and durability, making them ideal for exposed applications. Both types are widely used in roofing and waterproofing projects, providing reliable protection against water infiltration and environmental elements.</p></p>
