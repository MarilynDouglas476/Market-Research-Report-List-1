<p><p>The industrial laser cutting system market is categorized into several types, each serving distinct applications. Laser melting and cutting involves melting the material to create clean edges, ideal for metals. Laser oxygen cutting enhances efficiency by using oxygen to assist in cutting, particularly for thick materials.
