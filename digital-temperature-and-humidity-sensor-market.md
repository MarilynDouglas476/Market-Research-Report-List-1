<p><p>The digital temperature and humidity sensor market is categorized into two main types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals that represent temperature and humidity levels, making them suitable for simple monitoring applications. Digital output sensors, on the other hand, transmit precise data in a digital format, allowing for more accurate measurements and easier integration with microcontrollers and digital systems, making them ideal for advanced applications in industrial and consumer markets.</p></p>
