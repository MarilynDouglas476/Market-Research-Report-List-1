<p><p>An electro-fusion coupler is a specialized fitting used in the joining of polyethylene pipes, primarily in gas and water distribution systems. The increasing demand for reliable and efficient pipeline solutions, particularly in urban plumbing and gas distribution, drives the market for electro-fusion couplers.</p><p>The Electro-fusion Coupler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
