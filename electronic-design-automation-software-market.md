<p><p>The Electronic Design Automation (EDA) software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EDA offers flexibility, accessibility, and scalability, allowing users to access tools and resources over the internet, which facilitates collaboration and reduces infrastructure costs. On-premise EDA, on the other hand, involves software installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control, security, and potentially better performance for complex designs. Each type caters to distinct organizational needs and preferences in electronic design processes.</p></p>
