<p><p>The battery-free wireless sensor tags market is categorized into three types: active tags, passive tags, and semi-active tags. Active tags generate their own power, enabling longer ranges and enhanced functionality. Passive tags rely on external sources, such as RFID readers, to function, making them cost-effective for short-range applications. Semi-active tags combine characteristics of both, using a small battery for some functions while remaining powered by external sources for communication, offering a balance of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
