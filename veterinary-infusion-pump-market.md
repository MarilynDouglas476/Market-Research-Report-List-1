<p><p>The veterinary infusion pump market is segmented into large-volume infusion pumps and syringe infusion pumps. Large-volume infusion pumps are designed to deliver significant fluid volumes, suitable for critical care situations where hydration or medication delivery is needed over extended periods. Conversely, syringe infusion pumps are compact devices that provide precise control for smaller volumes, ideal for administering medications in delicate cases or when dosage accuracy is paramount. Both types enhance patient care in veterinary practices by ensuring consistent and reliable fluid administration.</p></p>
