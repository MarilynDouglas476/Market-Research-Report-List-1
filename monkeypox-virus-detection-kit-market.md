<p><p>The Monkeypox Virus Detection Kit market is primarily categorized into two types: Double PCR Detection and Triple PCR Assay. Double PCR Detection utilizes two sets of primers to amplify the viral DNA, offering reliable specificity and sensitivity for identifying the monkeypox virus. In contrast, Triple PCR Assay employs three sets of primers, enhancing detection accuracy and minimizing false negatives. Both methods are essential for effective surveillance, outbreak management, and timely diagnosis, supporting public health efforts against monkeypox.</p></p>
