<p><p>The laser micromachining market is divided primarily into UV and green laser types, each serving distinct applications. UV lasers utilize shorter wavelengths, making them ideal for precise cutting and engraving of delicate materials, including plastics and glass, without generating significant heat. Conversely, green lasers, with a wavelength suitable for processing metals and ceramics, excel in applications requiring deeper penetration and higher energy absorption. Both technologies are crucial for industries like electronics, medical device manufacturing, and microfabrication.</p></p>
