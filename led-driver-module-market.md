<p><p>The LED driver module market comprises three main types based on voltage levels: high voltage, low voltage, and medium voltage LED drivers. High voltage LED driver modules, typically operating over 100V, are used in applications like large signage and street lighting. Low voltage LED drivers, often under 60V, are favored in residential and decorative lighting. Medium voltage LED driver modules fall between the two, generally ranging from 60V to 100V, and are commonly employed in commercial lighting solutions, balancing efficiency and power consumption.</p></p>
