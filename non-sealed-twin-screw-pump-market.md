<p><p>The Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pump market primarily includes two types: Single Volute Twin Screw Pumps and Double Volute Twin Screw Pumps. Single Volute Pumps feature a single chamber for fluid flow, making them simpler and often more compact. In contrast, Double Volute Twin Screw Pumps have two chambers, which help distribute pressure more evenly, reducing vibration and enhancing efficiency. Both types are designed for handling various fluids in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, offering distinct advantages depending on the application.</p></p>
