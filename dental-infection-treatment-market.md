Periodontal abscess treatment addresses infections affecting the gums and supporting structures, typically involving scaling, root planing, or surgery. Other types encompass various dental infections, necessitating diverse treatment approaches like antibiotics, drainage procedures, or advanced surgical interventions tailored to the specific infection type.</p></p>
