<p><strong>The Double Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Break Isolator market is categorized into three main types: Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. </p><p>- **Bus Side** isolators are positioned between the busbars and the power equipment, ensuring safe maintenance. </p><p>- **Line Side** isolators connect the incoming power lines to the busbar, isolating faults from the network. </p><p>- **Transfer Bus Side** isolators facilitate a switch between two different bus systems, providing operational flexibility and ensuring continuity of service. Each type plays a crucial role in electrical system safety and reliability.</p></p>
