<p><p>Sanitary double seal valves are crucial in maintaining hygiene and preventing contamination in food and pharmaceutical industries. The market is primarily categorized into single seat and double seat types. Single seat valves feature a more compact design, offering efficient performance with minimal product retention, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent cleaning. In contrast, double seat valves consist of two separate seals and are designed for higher pressure and flow applications, ensuring better containment and safety during processing.</p></p>
