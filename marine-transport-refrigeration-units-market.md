<p><p>Marine transport refrigeration units are crucial for preserving perishable goods during transit. The market can be categorized into two types: open and semi-closed systems. Open refrigeration units directly expose the refrigerant to the environment, allowing for easy maintenance and cost-effectiveness but may have lower efficiency. In contrast, semi-closed systems utilize a sealed refrigerant cycle, improving efficiency and performance while reducing environmental exposure, making them suitable for larger vessels needing consistent temperature control during long journeys.</p></p>
