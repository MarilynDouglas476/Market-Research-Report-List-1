<p><p>Food irradiation sterilization solutions involve various radiation types to enhance food safety and extend shelf life. Gamma radiation uses cobalt-60 or cesium-137 sources to effectively kill bacteria and parasites. X-ray radiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to penetrate deeper into food products, ensuring uniform sterilization without significant heat. Electron beam radiation employs high-energy electrons for rapid treatment, making it suitable for low-dose applications. Each method offers unique benefits in food preservation, tailored to different products and consumer safety requirements.</p></p>
