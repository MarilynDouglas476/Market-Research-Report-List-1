<p><p>X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) coating thickness gauges are categorized into Common Type and Polycapillary Type. The Common Type utilizes a conventional X-ray source and detector, offering reliable measurement for various substrates and coatings. In contrast, the Polycapillary Type employs polycapillary optics to focus X-rays more efficiently, enhancing sensitivity and enabling analysis of thinner coatings or smaller areas. Both types are essential for industries requiring precise coating measurements, supporting quality control and material analysis in manufacturing and research applications.</p></p>
