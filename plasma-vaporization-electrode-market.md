<p><ul><li>Bipolar</li><li>Monopolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plasma Vaporization Electrode market can be segmented into two primary types: bipolar and monopolar electrodes. Bipolar electrodes utilize both electrodes at the target site, allowing for more localized treatment with reduced thermal spread, making them ideal for delicate procedures. In contrast, monopolar electrodes involve an active electrode at the treatment site and a grounding pad placed elsewhere, offering broader tissue treatment. Both types cater to varying surgical needs, influencing their market demand based on procedure types and clinical preferences.</p></p>
