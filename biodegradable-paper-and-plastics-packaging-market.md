<p><p>The biodegradable paper and plastics packaging market includes types such as PLA (polylactic acid) and PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates). PLA is derived from renewable resources like cornstarch and is widely used for packaging due to its compostability and thermoplastic properties. PHA, produced by microbial fermentation, offers superior biodegradability and is suitable for various applications. Both materials cater to the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions, addressing environmental concerns while providing alternatives to traditional plastics, thereby promoting a circular economy.</p></p>
